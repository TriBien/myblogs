My First Post

2026-04-05

Starting a blog is easy. Finishing one is hard. This is my attempt to write about things I actually understand.

The "First Principles" mindset

In a world of hot takes and surface-level tutorials, there's value in going deeper. Not for its own sake, but because the fundamentals don't change as often as the trendy wrappers do.

This blog isn't about being right. It's about being honest about what I know — and what I don't.

The best way to learn is to teach. The second best is to write about it.
